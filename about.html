About Us

We are a long-standing, family-owned marine
dealership in Nebraska.

Contact Us

Our story began in 1980, and for more than 40 years we’ve proudly served boaters across Nebraska and the Midwest. In 2000, ownership passed to Paul Davis - our second-generation owner - who continues the legacy his father started with the same passion for boating and dedication to our customers. Over the years, Omaha Marine Center has grown into a full-service marine dealership with expansive showrooms, state-of-the-art service facilities, and one of the largest parts and accessories departments in the region.

Culture & Values

We Are More Than a Store

At Omaha Marine Center, we see ourselves as more than just a boat dealership, we’re a family-first environment built on honesty, passion, and a love for having fun on the water. Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our customers through great boating experiences and exceptional service. We take pride in the expertise of our team - many of whom have been with us for years - and we’re committed to ongoing training, factory certification, and staying up to date with the latest marine-industry standards.

Community Involvement & Recognition

Boating Community Involvement

We're proud to be an active part of the Omaha and Nebraska boating community. Each year, we participate in local boat shows and events in the Omaha and Lincoln areas — great opportunities to connect with fellow boaters and share our passion for life on the water. Over the years, we've been honored with recognition such as being named one of North America's Top 100 Boat Dealers and earning "Best of Omaha" awards. With our full-service facility, expansive showroom, and one of the area's largest selections of parts and accessories, Omaha Marine Center has become a trusted hub for boaters throughout the Midwest.
