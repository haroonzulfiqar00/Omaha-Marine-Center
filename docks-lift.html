Boat Lifts

From hydraulic to manual

Whether you're looking for one of the safest, most dependable lifts on the market like the ShoreStation Hydraulic, or the nostalgia of our original manual drive lift, ShoreStation continues to elevate the industry standards.

  • Quickly Secures Your Boat: Our hydraulic lift is the fastest on the market, letting you safely land and secure your boat even in windy or rough conditions.
  • Maximum Protection: With a cable-pulling system that prevents fatigue and pressure-regulated hydraulics that stall before overload, your boat stays protected from damage.
  • Reliable by Design: Built for longevity, the “high and dry” design keeps key components above water, reducing moisture exposure. A solar-powered DC system ensures safe, dependable performance without needing dockside AC power.
