KEY POINTS

We Offer Consignment and Cash Buy Bid Options on Boats

Boats must be less than 15 years old and in good mechanical condition.
We do not accept:
  • Boats older than 15 years
  • Large cruisers or jet boats
  • Boats without trailers
  • Project boats or loose outboard motors
  • Boats with Volvo, Johnson, Suzuki, Honda, or Evinrude motors
Consignment season: March 1 - July 15 (no exceptions)
Required documents: Clear free Nebraska boat title and trailer title and/or current trailer registration.
