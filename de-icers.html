Kasco Oil-Free De-Icers and Bubblers

Our Staff Offers A Combined Total Of 225 Years Of Marine Repair Experience!

Protect your dock, boat, and waterfront property this winter with Kasco oil-free de-icers, the trusted choice for reliable ice prevention. Designed and engineered in the U.S.A., Kasco De-Icers are built to perform in the harshest marine environments and are the industry standard for keeping water open and ice-free. By circulating warmer water from below the surface, these systems help prevent costly damage from ice expansion, jacking, and lifting, while also reducing winter fish kill caused by low oxygen and ice cover.
